HELLO THERE!

I'm Nora, an Adult Bachelors student at The New School/Parsons pursuing a B.S. Liberal Arts with a concentration in Digital Design. I'm passionate about user experience design, interaction design, the intersection of design and technology, and designing for social impact. When I'm not designing, I enjoy making things (especially bikes and 3D printed accessories), discovering new cultures, drinking fancy teas, eating Indian food, and exploring the many strange corners of New York City.

I am currently finishing my coursework while working as a freelance designer and a 3D and laser cutting lab assistant at The New School/Parson's Making Center. The following is a final portfolio of my work from Web Design I, a 9 week course focused on designing and building websites using HTML and CSS.
