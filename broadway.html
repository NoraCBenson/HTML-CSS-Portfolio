BEST OF BROADWAY

Best of Broadway was a imitation cheaptickets.com recommendation list I made for homework after our week 3 class. This exercise gave me the chance to try out some of the new HTML techniques we had learned in class like adding meter bars and embedding videos. I also began using stylesheets and basic CSS to start adding color, styling, and different fonts to the page. I embedded a Broadway style font for the headers and added a dotted gold border to the title "Nora's Best of Broadway" to make it look like a Broadway sign. I also included video clips for each of my recommendations so vistors could get a taste of what each show was like. Want to check out some of these great performances? Visit my site here!
