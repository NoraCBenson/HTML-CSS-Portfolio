ALL ABOUT HOMER

All About Homer was a site I made in the first week of our class as a way to practice some of the basics of HTML/CSS. I used h1, p, img, b, figure, ol, and li tags to create a bare-bones site about the classic cartoon father figure, Homer Simpson. I came back later in the course and added a stylesheet which I then used to make things more readable and add some pizzaz to the page.
